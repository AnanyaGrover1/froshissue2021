Dear ’25,

As you begin your Princeton journey, we would like to introduce ourselves. The Daily Princetonian is the only daily news publication covering the University and the town of Princeton. Our paper — 300 students strong — is student-run, not-for-profit, and financially independent from the University. We keep you up-to-date and strive to share the stories of your fellow Princetonians.

Over the next few weeks, we’ll share pieces from the ‘Prince’ that we think you’ll find worthwhile — articles about where to eat around campus, how student activists have inspired change, and more on this student-curated website. Be sure to check back here!
